Welcome to Online National Election Services

Available services

Access Registration Page:
Visit https://portal.election.go.ke/. Look for a "Sign Up," "Register,". Provide Basic Information: Fill out a registration form with basic information such as: Full Name Email Address Password Verify Email: click Register.

Accessing the Voting Platform:

Once registered and verified, Log in to the online voting platform using your credentials. Make your selections by marking your choices on the online ballot. Review the list of candidates selected and submit your selections.
